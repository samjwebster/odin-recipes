Slightly Unstable Potion

Description

Create the most perfect, and definitely most safe, Slightly Unstable Potion following this recipe! Let its bubbles overwhelm your tastebuds as it sinks you into an unstoppable dream. The particulate instability and inconceivable flavor adds to the fun!

Warning: consume at your own discretion. This concotion has not been approved by the Potion Crafter's Association (PCA). We are not liable to any physical and/or spiritual damages nor potential emotional torture you may endure while consuming our Slightly Unstable Potion.

Ingredients

  • A clear, unscratched vessel to hold the liquid
  • Unfiltered spring water
  • Long-forgotten childhood memories
  • Animal tears (preferably rodent)
  • 1/4 cup of crushed maple leaf
  • Miscellaneous trinkets
  • Potent dandelion seeds
  • 1-2 orbs of light
  • Feathers of a bird that doesn't yet exist
  • Patience

Steps

Preparation

  1. Begin by preparing your vessel. Rinse the inside using an even mixture of spring water and animal tears.
  2. Ponder the childhood memories for 3-5 minutes. Fresher memories concoct a longer-lasting potion.
  3. Perform a genetic test on the feathers to ensure that the bird they are from has yet to exist. Note: feathers from a bird that does exist will cause you to cease existing. Proceed with caution!

Brewing

  1. Add the 1/4 cup of crushed maple leaf and a sprinkle of potent dandelion seeds. Shake vigorously. Stop when evenly mixed to avoid pulping during brewing process.
  2. Place your vessel onto a flat surface. Arrange the miscellaneous trinkets around the vessel in a geometric shape of your choosing.
  3. Begin drizzling the maple-dandelion mixture with the animal tears. Once the trinkets begin to float, stop pouring.
  4. Without disturbing the floating trinkets, begin adding unfiltered spring water. The trinkets should now start lowering down. Cease pouring spring water as soon as they touch down to the surface.
  5. Collect the trinkets and burn them. (Feel free to complete this step after brewing!)
  6. Around the vessel, add your orbs of light (to taste). Allow the concoction to absorb the light for 10 minutes.
  7. After absorption is completed, drape a tattered cloth over the orbs of light. Place your vessel into a dark cabinet. Lock it from the inside and burn the key.
  8. Wait 1-3 years. Once completed, the cabinet will unlock itself.
  9. Viola! Now enjoy your perfect slightly unstable potion!
