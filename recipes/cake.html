Glowing Sponge Cake

Description

Try this delicious Glowing Sponge Cake to impress your friend's and family's tastebuds. This cake is not only delicious on its own but makes a fantastic base for your future glowing cakes. Not suitable for ingenstion by children under 10 years old, as it may cause glowing skin.

Ingedients

  • 1500 hummingbird eggs
  • 2 cups of granulated glow-sugar
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat a lava-fuel oven to 2000 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In two large vats, separate the yolk and whites of all 1500 robin eggs. Add a half cup of glow-sugar to each vat and stir until thoroughly mixed. Combine the yolks and whites, but do not stir.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder haphazardly. You will notice that the mixture will begin to homogenize.
  4. Pour your now mixed cake batter into a lined cake pan. Add the cake pan to the oven and allow it to bake at 700 degrees for 10 seconds. Remove from the oven.
  5. Bon appetit! Turn off the lights and enjoy your Glowing Sponge Cake!
